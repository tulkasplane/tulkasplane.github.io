Curriculum Overview

This schedule facilitates interdisciplinary collaboration between students from the Stanford Graduate School of Business (GSB) and partners from SLS, MIP, CEE, and SOM. To maximize the educational experience, groups are rotated each session to minimize repeat pairings and foster a diverse network of perspectives.
